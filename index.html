📋 Terms & Conditions
Please read before using CTU Grade Calculator

1. For Reference Only

This tool is for personal academic reference only. Results are not official and must not be used as a substitute for your official grade records from CTU Carmen Campus.

2. Data Privacy

Your uploaded grade sheet is processed by Google Gemini AI to extract grade data. The developer (Raymar B. Sulib) does NOT have access to your uploaded files or grade data. Files are processed in real-time and are never stored on any server.

3. Accuracy

AI extraction may occasionally have errors. Always verify results against your official CTU records. GWA and honor computations follow CTU Carmen Campus guidelines to the best of the developer's knowledge.

4. Saved Data

Your extracted grades may be saved locally on your device (browser storage only) for your convenience when you return. This data never leaves your device.

5. No Liability

The developer is not responsible for any academic or personal decisions made based on the results shown by this tool.
